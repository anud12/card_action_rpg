# Zones

## Mine (4 cards deep)

|Name| Count | Decription|
|-|-|-|
| Patrol | 1 | Draw 2 [enemy] in a line
| Wild beast nest | 1 | Draw 1 beast [enemy]
| Passageway | 1 | Draw 1 [zone], if it has enemies draw to lure one
| Guard Post | 1 | Draw 1 [enemy], if defeated may replace zone deck with 1 [hideout deck]
| Glittering Vein | 1 | Test [mining] 1 [ore]
| Rich Seam | 1 | Test [mining] for 2 [ore]

| Booby-Trapped Passage | 1 | Test [initiative] and if fail draw 1 [hideout]
