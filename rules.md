Rules for a card based action rpg

- players each have a character
- must achieve different objectives

Turn Sequence

- Start of Round - All enemies refresh their available actions.
- Action Phase - Players take turns activating any character, one at a time:
  - Enemies: Select an applicable action from their card, move according to its pattern, and immediately resolve its effect.
  - Players: Assign Time cards and resolve abilities, movement, and equipment effects.
- End of Round - Once all characters have activated, return all spent Time cards to the Time Deck and reshuffle.

Abilities cards

The player can have abilities cards which denotes what actions the player can take, the starting ones are:

| Melee Strike | 1x Time | Draw 3 weapon cards |
| Move | 1x Time | Move 1 hex |

Time deck

At the start of your turn, draw cards from your Time Deck.

Spend drawn Time cards to pay the Time Cost of abilities or equipment.

At the end of the round, return all spent cards to the deck and reshuffle.

Starting Time Deck:

| Name | Count | Extra draw |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Steady | 4   | 0   |
| Swifty | 1   | 1   |

Health deck

Each character has a Health Deck that represents their vitality.

When the character is attacked, resolve each attack by revealing cards from the top of the Health Deck until their wound symbols meet or exceed the attack's wound value.

If a character's health deck is empty, it is defeated.
